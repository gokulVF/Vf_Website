    Cookies

    Our websites use "cookies" to provide various services to you. A cookie is a small piece of data that a website sends to your browser and is stored on your computer or device. There are two main types of cookies,

    1. Session cookies.
    2. Persistent cookies.

    Session cookies are temporary and disappear when you leave our site, while persistent cookies stay on your device for a set period.

    Cookies help our websites work better with your device. Vacation Feast uses cookies to gather information for different purposes, like improving how our websites run, understanding what you like, customizing our site for you, and making our ads more relevant. Sometimes, other companies may also use our cookies to show you ads on other websites.

    You can manage cookies using your browser settings. This means you can delete cookies, choose not to accept them, or get notified when a website is using them.
