Lucerne

Embark on an exhilarating journey to Mt. Titlis, ascending to 3,020 meters via the Rotair, the world's first revolving cable car. Enjoy breathtaking 360-degree views and explore the Cliff Walk, Europe's highest suspension bridge. Then, experience the charm of Lucerne with an orientation tour, including iconic landmarks like the Lion Monument and Kapellbrücke. Conclude the day with The Swiss Chocolate Adventure, delving into Switzerland's rich chocolate heritage. Overnight in Central Switzerland.

Innsbruck

Relax on a scenic drive to Innsbruck, passing through Vaduz, the capital of Liechtenstein, known for its stunning Alpine scenery and policy of neutrality. Experience a guided mini train ride in Vaduz, offering serene views of the picturesque landscapes. Explore the Swarovski World of Crystals in Wattens, marveling at exquisite crystalline artworks. With the majestic Alps as a backdrop, immerse yourself in the rich culture of medieval Europe. Overnight in Innsbruck / Seefeld.

After checking out from your hotel, we'll journey to Pisa, where you'll marvel at the iconic Leaning Tower of Pisa in the Square of Miracles.
